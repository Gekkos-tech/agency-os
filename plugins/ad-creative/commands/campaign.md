Ideate a campaign for: $ARGUMENTS

Load the `ad-concepts` skill and follow its process:

1. Audience-insight step first (pain, desire, objection, trigger moment) —
   state it in 4 lines before any ideas.
2. Deliver exactly **3 campaign routes** with different emotional registers,
   each with: working title, core claim, hook, visual idea, primary channel
   + why, and the framework used.
3. Close with a recommendation: which route to present first to the client
   and what to validate before production.

If the user picks a route afterwards, continue with `ad-copy` (headlines,
CTAs, A/B variants) and `social-formats` (format matrix) — offer both.
